Body text is Titillium Web Regular 28px with 32px of line height. The next element is a frame, we have so many uses for it, like for instance, featuring an area of information or interaction.

Many times we won't have space enough in the screen as to show the entire block of info. That's why we will open a frame in the panel and use a scroll to see all the content.

It is important to use the panel frame and the scroll together, because we need an excuse to hide the scrollable content somewhere behind the panel.
